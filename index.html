Traveler's Guide

Everything a newcomer needs to know

How do I enter the Tavern? ▾

Click the "Enter the Tavern" button at the top of this page, or navigate directly to timelesstavern.pub. You'll be greeted by the login screen where you can access your account. If you're new and need credentials, reach out to the Tavern keeper through our Discord community.

What is SillyTavern? ▾

SillyTavern is a powerful, open-source interface for interacting with AI language models. It provides a rich environment for creative writing, roleplay, and storytelling with features like character cards, lorebooks, group chats, and much more. Timeless Tavern is our hosted instance, so you don't need to set up anything yourself.

What can I do here? ▾

The possibilities are vast:

  • Create and roleplay with AI characters in any genre or setting
  • Build detailed worlds with lorebooks and persistent memory
  • Join group adventures with other community members
  • Use creative writing tools to develop stories, scripts, and more
  • Participate in community events and writing challenges
Rules of the Tavern ▾

We keep it simple — be respectful, be creative, and be kind. This is a shared space for storytelling and imagination. Treat fellow travelers as you'd want to be treated. Any issues or concerns should be brought to the Tavern keeper directly.

Tips for new travelers ▾

Start small. Pick a character or scenario that interests you and dive in. Don't worry about getting everything perfect on your first try — the best stories come from exploration and experimentation. Check out the community Discord for tips from experienced travelers, and don't hesitate to ask questions.
